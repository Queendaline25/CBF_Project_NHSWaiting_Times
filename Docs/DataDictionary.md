### Glossary of Data Terms used in project
| Name | Definition|
| ------------- | ------------- |
| RTT |Referal to Treatment  |
| Admitted  | RTT waiting times for cases where patients completed their RTT pathway during the month with an inpatient/day case admission for treatment  |
|Non-Admitted|RTT waiting times for cases where patients completed their RTT pathway during the month for reasons other than an inpatient/day case admission for treatment 
|95th percentile |This is the time that in 95% of cases the patient had waited less than to start treatment (and 5% of cases the patient had waited more than)
|Treatment Function| RTT waiting times are measured within 19 treatment functions.Treatment functions are based on specialties
|% within 18 weeks|The percentage of RTT periods within 18 weeks of referral.
| % Winthin 52 weeks| The percentage of RTT periods within 52 weeks of referal.
| Median Average|The  middle points of all RTT waits
|Provider|An organisation that provides NHS treatment or care, for example an NHS Trust
